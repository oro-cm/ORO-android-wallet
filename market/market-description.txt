<b>Have your Oros always with you, in your pocket!</b> You pay by quickly scanning a QR code. As a merchant, you receive payments reliably and instantly. Oro Wallet is the first mobile Oro app, and arguably also the most secure!


<b>FEATURES</b>

• No registration, web service or cloud needed! This wallet is de-centralized and peer to peer.
• Display of Oro amount in ORO, mORO and µORO.
• Conversion to and from national currencies.
• Sending and receiving of Oro via NFC, QR codes or Oro URLs.
• Address book for regularly used Oro addresses.
• When you're offline, you can still pay via Bluetooth.
• System notification for received coins.
• Sweeping of paper wallets (e.g. those used for cold storage).
• App widget for Oro balance.
